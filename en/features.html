Features

SCHEDULES

Uncomplicate scheduling control

  • Shared calendar
  • Creating a schedule with 1 click
  • Available for appointment 24 hours a day 7 days a week
  • Color system that allows the distinction of the stages of the agenda
  • Automatic synchronization of manual and automatic schedules (App or Website)
  • Blocking days or times at the unit and professional level
SERVICE ORDERS & COMMANDS

Your sales and service in one place

  • Sale of services, products and packages directly on the Service Order
  • Manual discounts
  • Application of pre-defined discounts (vouchers)
  • Payment division in different ways (cash, card and etc.)
  • Automatic credit accumulation (loyalty points and service packages)
  • Payment by credits (loyalty points or services purchased by packages)
BOX CONTROL

Have total control of the financial day-to-day

  • Detailed box control (with opening and closing)
  • Automatic calculation of professional commissions
  • Manual or pre-registered discounts
  • Control of payment methods
  • Full details of all financial transactions
  • Breaks and possible errors automatically highlighted
